Córdoba

Day Trip · Saturday, July 18

La Mezquita

A mosque-cathedral that defies easy description. Eight hundred red-and-white arches stretching into near-infinity — one of humanity's supreme architectural achievements.

La Judería

The medieval Jewish quarter — a labyrinth of whitewashed lanes, flower-draped courtyards, and the quiet hum of centuries. Don't miss the ancient Synagogue.

Alcázar Gardens

Terraced Moorish gardens with Roman mosaics and reflecting pools. The perfect afternoon cool-down before the train back south to Sevilla.

Getting Around

Transport Notes

Madrid Airport → City

Airport Express Bus Line 203

Yellow bus from Terminal T1. €5 each. Drops at Atocha station, runs 24/7 every 15–20 min. Journey ~40 min. No bags fee. Best option on arrival Day 2.

Spain Internal Rail

Renfe AVE High-Speed

All mainland legs are AVE except Sevilla–Córdoba (45 min regional). Book ahead on Renfe.com — prices rise closer to travel. Madrid–Sevilla ~2h 20m · Sevilla–Granada ~2h 38m · Granada–Madrid ~3h 36m.

Ibiza Arrival

Palladium Rewards Shuttle

Free shared shuttle from airport to TRS Ibiza Hotel as part of the Palladium Rewards booking. May have a short wait for other guests. Coordinate with hotel on arrival time.

Madrid Final Night

Meliá T4 — No Transfer Needed

Meliá Madrid Aeropuerto is directly connected to Terminal T4 via walkway. No taxi or shuttle required on departure morning. Check-in to T4 directly from hotel.

Before You Go

Key Risks & Reminders

⚠ Book Immediately

Alhambra Tickets

Sells out weeks ahead in July. Limited to 300 visitors per half-hour slot — Nasrid Palaces fill first. Book on alhambra.org as soon as possible. Hotel Alhambra Palace is a short walk to the entrance which helps, but it won't get you a slot.

⚠ Long Transit Day

Day 11 — Granada → Madrid → Ibiza

This is a full transit day. Don't book tight connections. Allow ample buffer between the AVE arriving at Atocha and the Madrid → Ibiza flight at Barajas. The metro/taxi between Atocha and T4 takes 45–60 min including security.

⚠ Morning After

Day 15 — Ibiza Flight Timing

Amnesia runs until sunrise on Sat Jul 25. Do NOT book an early morning Ibiza → Madrid flight. Book afternoon or evening departure — Iberia has high Saturday frequency. The Meliá T4 connection means no rush once you're in Madrid.
