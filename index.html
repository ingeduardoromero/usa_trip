Córdoba

Day Trip · Saturday, July 18

La Mezquita

A mosque-cathedral that defies easy description. Eight hundred red-and-white arches stretching into near-infinity — one of humanity's supreme architectural achievements.

La Judería

The medieval Jewish quarter — a labyrinth of whitewashed lanes, flower-draped courtyards, and the quiet hum of centuries. Don't miss the ancient Synagogue.

Alcázar Gardens

Terraced Moorish gardens with Roman mosaics and reflecting pools. The perfect afternoon cool-down before the train back south to Sevilla.

Days 9 – 11

Granada

2 nights  ·  July 18 – 20, 2026

The Alhambra rises above the city like a crown of rust and gold. Below, the Albaicín's whitewashed maze spills down the hillside with views that stop you mid-breath. Granada is the soul of Moorish Spain — and we stay on the hill itself, steps from the palace.

Wander the Albaicín by morning, climb to Mirador de San Nicolás for the view. Tapas in the afternoon — every drink comes with a free plate. The city eases you in gently.

Days 11 – 15

Ibiza

4 nights  ·  July 21 – 25, 2026

The Balearic island that invented a feeling. Crystal water, pine forests, ancient sunsets from Dalt Vila, and the best sound systems in the world. We come for the beaches, the food, and one completely unforgettable night at Amnesia.
